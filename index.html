    Real Love

    I’ve never been one for soppy romance. I balk at all the Nicholas Sparks books. I can’t keep quiet when watching a Rom-Com. They're filled with cliché meet-cutes, predictable misunderstanding and declarations of love. That’s not to say that I don’t like love. I love love. I love love when it's stripped down to the bare essentials, when it comes to the quiet displays of care and attention. Now-a-days it’s all about being whisked away on surprise, jet-setting adventures by your billionaire boyfriend on your first date or waking up to a room filled with single-stem roses....
